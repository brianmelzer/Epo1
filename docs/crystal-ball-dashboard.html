01The two loops

The inner loop predicts what will win; the outer loop is the real business. They are coupled: the inner loop's Recommend feeds the outer loop's Call-out, and the outer loop's commercial outcomes (IQS) flow back to retrain the inner loop. That feedback is what makes call-out quality compound. The dots show flow direction; live numbers sit on the key nodes.
